About Us

BASH EMIR — is an international oil trading structure engaged in the trade petroleum products.

Our company procures and supplies a wide range of petroleum products internationally. We work with reliable suppliers in Russia, China, India, Iran, Kazakhstan, Azerbaijan, Turkmenistan, Turkey. The company has all the necessary resources for timely transactions around the world. Thanks to many years of experience in the industry, we have fine-tuned the supply process from the manufacturer to the end consumer, ensuring the efficiency, safety, and sustainability of their interaction.
